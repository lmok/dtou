Introduction

This work focuses on allowing people control how their personal information, (that is, information created by them, about them, toward them, or for them) should be handled in situations where there are many separate data controllers.

Note that this is not a framework for guaranteeing the enforcement of such preferences; but for supporting the expression of such preferences and allowing people to easily comply with them if they choose (for politeness), or to be able to easily understand the ways that their uses of such information violates actorsâ€™ intents.
